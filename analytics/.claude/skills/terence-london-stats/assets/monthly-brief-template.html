Daily visits

The website is doing its job: more than a third of visitors click to book, mostly from their phones, mostly found through Google. The growth room now is in filling the quiet days and getting social working.
Where to focus next
1

Connect clicks to real bookings

37 people clicked "Book" — the next question is how many became confirmed appointments. If your booking system can share that, we can report actual bookings, not just intent.

2

Turn on Instagram

Social sends almost no traffic today, yet it's where salon clients discover stylists. Regular before/after posts and reels, with the booking link in bio, is the biggest untapped source of new visitors.

3

Repeat the 19 May spike

That day tripled the usual traffic. If it traces to a post or promotion, it's a simple playbook for quieter weeks.

4

Keep feeding Google

Google drives most visits and bookings. A complete, photo-rich Google Business Profile with fresh reviews keeps that pipeline strong — and it compounds over time.
