Recommendations
1

Add pages for /about, /contact, /book, /team

People are typing these URLs and hitting 404s. Either create these pages or add redirects to the relevant sections of the homepage.

2

Fix the Facebook link strategy

100% bounce rate means every Facebook click is wasted effort. Check the link preview, test the landing experience from Facebook, and consider whether the audience targeting matches.

3

Post at 6 pm to catch the evening peak

Site traffic peaks between 7–9 pm. Posting content or promotions at 6 pm gives the algorithm time to surface it right when your audience is browsing.

4

Run a Thursday promotion

Thursday is the quietest day by far (9 sessions vs 44 on Tuesday). A mid-week special or targeted post on Wednesday evening could fill this gap.

5

Double down on local SEO

Add Templestowe/Melbourne-specific schema markup, keep Google Business Profile updated with photos and reviews, and consider location-specific landing page content.
